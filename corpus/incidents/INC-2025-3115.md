# INC-2025-3115 — Metal Detector MIL-MD-5500-01, calibration drift

- **Date:** 2025-01-04
- **Line / Equipment:** QC-Line-X / Metal Detector (Quality Control)
- **Severity:** Low
- **Product affected:** Cinnamon Rolls (QC stage)

## Symptoms
False-reject rate doubled over a week. Signal-to-noise ratio drifted by 8 dB.
No mechanical, thermal, or vibration symptoms — purely a calibration drift on
the search head.

## Root cause
Ambient EMI from a nearby VFD on a newly commissioned conveyor was coupling
into the search-head antenna, drifting the calibration set point.

## Corrective action
Re-zero the search head with a test card, install an EMI filter on the VFD's
output cable, and re-balance the rejector air-blast timing.

## Impact
- **Downtime:** 0.7 hours (off-line calibration)
- **Cost:** $620

## Keywords
Metal Detector, Quality Control, calibration drift, EMI, false reject rate,
signal-to-noise.
