# INC-2025-0317 — Tunnel Oven TO-3, Zone 2 heating element burnout

- **Date:** 2025-03-17
- **Line / Equipment:** Line 3 / Tunnel Oven XB-2400 (TO-3), Zone 2
- **Severity:** Critical
- **Product affected:** Naan

## Symptoms
Uneven browning on the **top surface, leading edge** of naan. Operators reported
hot spots and inconsistent color across the band. Zone 2 current draw had drifted
**+11%** over the prior shift; thermocouple variance climbed from ~2 °C² to ~9 °C².
Vibration baseline remained normal, ruling out a mechanical fault.

## Root cause
**Top heating element coil degradation** from repeated thermal cycling. The
degraded coil drew higher current while delivering uneven radiant heat, producing
localized over-browning on the product top surface.

## Corrective action
Replaced Zone 2 top element (part **HE-2400-Z2**, torque 18 N·m on the bus-bar
terminals) and recalibrated the Zone 2 thermocouple pair. Verified current draw
returned to 42 ± 0.5 A baseline.

## Impact
- **Downtime:** 4.2 hours
- **Cost:** $18,400 (parts + labor + scrapped product)
- **MTTR class:** planned replacement is ~4.8× cheaper than emergency repair.

oven, zone 2, heating element, element coil degradation, uneven browning, top
