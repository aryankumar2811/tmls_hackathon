# INC-2025-0204 — Cooling Conveyor C-Series, belt tracking drift

- **Date:** 2025-02-04
- **Line / Equipment:** Line 2 / Cooling Conveyor C-Series
- **Severity:** Medium
- **Product affected:** Burger buns

## Symptoms
**Belt alignment offset** drifted from ~1 mm to >10 mm, pulling product toward one
edge. Drive **motor current** rose ~2.5 A as the belt rubbed the frame. Buns showed
**position offset** and uneven bake from sitting off-center under the cooling zone.

## Root cause
**Belt tracking drift** caused by an out-of-adjustment tail-pulley and worn edge
guide. Left unattended, tracking drift leads to belt-edge fraying and eventual belt
failure within 8–12 hours.

## Corrective action
Re-squared the tail pulley, adjusted the tracking screws to center the belt, and
replaced the worn edge guide (part **CG-CS-04**). Alignment held at 1.0 ± 0.5 mm.

## Impact
- **Downtime:** 1.6 hours
- **Cost:** $4,200

## Keywords
conveyor, C-Series, belt tracking drift, belt alignment offset, motor current,
position offset, uneven bake, tail pulley, edge guide.
