# INC-2025-0102 — Packaging Line PK-9, jaw seal wear

- **Date:** 2025-01-12
- **Line / Equipment:** Line 5 / Packaging Line PK-9
- **Severity:** Low
- **Product affected:** Sliced loaf bags

## Symptoms
Intermittent **incomplete seals** on the flow-wrapper; 2–5% of bags failed the seal
integrity check. Jaw temperature was nominal; the issue tracked with **jaw seal
wear** on the heated bars.

## Root cause
Worn sealing jaws (part **SJ-PK9**) no longer applied even pressure across the seal
band.

## Corrective action
Replaced the upper and lower sealing jaws and re-zeroed the pressure cam.

## Impact
- **Downtime:** 0.9 hours
- **Cost:** $1,900

## Keywords
packaging, PK-9, jaw seal wear, incomplete seal, flow wrapper, product waste.
