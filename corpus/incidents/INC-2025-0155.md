# INC-2025-0155 — Proofer P-12, humidity sensor drift

- **Date:** 2025-01-21
- **Line / Equipment:** Line 4 / Proofer P-12
- **Severity:** High
- **Product affected:** Dinner rolls

## Symptoms
Chamber **humidity** reading drifted down (82% → 64% RH) while **steam pressure**
fell from 1.4 to 0.95 bar; chamber temp crept up ~3 °C. Rolls came out
**under-proofed** with low rise and a tight, cracked crust.

## Root cause
**Humidity sensor drift** combined with a partially clogged steam injector. The
controller, reading a falsely low humidity, under-corrected and let the chamber dry
out, producing inconsistent fermentation.

## Corrective action
Replaced the humidity probe (part **HS-P12-RH**), descaled the steam injector
nozzle, and recalibrated the controller setpoint to 82% RH. Verified rise on a
test batch.

## Impact
- **Downtime:** 2.4 hours
- **Cost:** $7,600

## Keywords
proofer, P-12, humidity sensor drift, steam pressure, under-proofed, low rise,
fermentation, chamber humidity, steam injector.
