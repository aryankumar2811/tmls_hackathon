# INC-2025-3210 — Servo Depositor BRA-DEP-4100-02, dosing-piston seal failure

- **Date:** 2025-01-09
- **Line / Equipment:** PSY-Line-C / Servo Depositor (Depositing)
- **Severity:** Medium
- **Product affected:** Cupcake batter

## Symptoms
Deposit-weight variance went from ± 1 % to ± 6 %. Pressure on the dosing
manifold dropped 12 % under load. No vibration, no thermal issues. Defect rate
on the downstream packaging line climbed because over- and under-filled cups
were rejected.

## Root cause
Worn piston seal (**SEAL-DEP-4100**) allowed batter to bypass the dosing
chamber, varying deposit weight.

## Corrective action
Replace **SEAL-DEP-4100** and the wiper ring. Re-zero the load cell. Run a
50-cup test batch to verify deposit weight within ± 1 %.

## Impact
- **Downtime:** 1.4 hours
- **Cost:** $1,650

## Keywords
Servo Depositor, Depositing, dosing piston, seal failure, deposit weight,
SEAL-DEP-4100.
