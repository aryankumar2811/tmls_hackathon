# INC-2025-3406 — Spiral Freezer VAU-FRZ-9100-01, scheduled lubrication

- **Date:** 2025-01-18
- **Line / Equipment:** DON-Line-B / Spiral Freezer (Freezing)
- **Severity:** Low
- **Product affected:** Donuts

## Symptoms
Bearing temperature on the spiral drum drive crept from 38 °C to **42 °C**.
Conveyor speed showed a slight ripple. Vibration was nominal. Controller raised
**E1003 — Lubrication reminder triggered** at the 40-day interval.

## Root cause
Scheduled lubrication interval reached on the GYRoCOMPACT drum drive bearing —
no fault, predictive-maintenance signal only.

## Corrective action
Lubricate the drum drive bearing (**BRG-FRZ-9100-DRV**) with the low-temp food-
grade grease specified by JBT FoodTech. Inspect the door seals and the
evaporator coil frost pattern while access is open. Reset the timer.

## Impact
- **Downtime:** 1.0 hour
- **Cost:** $720

## Keywords
Spiral Freezer, Freezing, VAU-FRZ-9100-01, GYRoCOMPACT, drum drive bearing,
lubrication, E1003, BRG-FRZ-9100-DRV, low-temp food-grade grease, evaporator
coil inspection.
