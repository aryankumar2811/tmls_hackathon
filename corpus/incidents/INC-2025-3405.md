# INC-2025-3405 — Modular Belt Conveyor MIL-CNV-1300-01, lubrication overdue

- **Date:** 2025-01-22
- **Line / Equipment:** CKE-Line-A / Modular Belt Conveyor (Conveying)
- **Severity:** Low
- **Product affected:** Muffins

## Symptoms
Vibration RMS held at **2.0 mm/s** (mild elevation, well below seizure
thresholds), bearing temperature ticked up by 6 °C. Defect rate held near
baseline. Controller raised **E1003 — Lubrication reminder triggered** at the
scheduled 150-day mark, exactly as designed.

## Root cause
Routine lubrication interval was reached. No actual fault — the alert is the
preventive-maintenance signal. The slight vibration rise is normal "drift
toward service due" behavior.

## Corrective action
Apply food-grade NLGI-2 grease to the drive-end and tail-pulley bearings
(**BRG-CNV-1300-DE**, **BRG-CNV-1300-TP**). Inspect the modular belt for cracked
links. Log the service and reset the lubrication timer.

## Impact
- **Downtime:** 0.6 hours (planned PM)
- **Cost:** $480
