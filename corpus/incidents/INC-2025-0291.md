# INC-2025-0291 — Spiral Mixer SM-600, drive-end bearing wear

- **Date:** 2025-02-28
- **Line / Equipment:** Line 1 / Spiral Mixer SM-600
- **Severity:** High
- **Product affected:** Bread dough

## Symptoms
Rising **vibration RMS** (1.2 → 2.5 mm/s over two shifts) accompanied by elevated
**bearing temperature** (54 → 69 °C) and a small increase in mixer load. Finished
loaves showed **dense, under-developed crumb** from inconsistent dough development.

## Root cause
**Drive-end bearing wear** (part **BRG-600-DE**). Lubricant had degraded past its
service interval, accelerating raceway wear and introducing vibration into the
mixing action.

## Corrective action
Replaced the drive-end bearing, flushed and re-greased the housing with the
specified food-grade lubricant, and re-tensioned the drive belt. Vibration returned
to 1.2 mm/s baseline.

## Impact
- **Downtime:** 3.1 hours
- **Cost:** $11,800
- Caught before bearing seizure (2–4 week window from first alert).

## Keywords
spiral mixer, SM-600, bearing wear, drive-end bearing, vibration RMS, bearing
