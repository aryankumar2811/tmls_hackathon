# INC-2025-0078 — Depositor D-4, nozzle clog

- **Date:** 2025-01-06
- **Line / Equipment:** Line 1 / Depositor D-4
- **Severity:** Low
- **Product affected:** Muffin batter

## Symptoms
Inconsistent **deposit weight** across cups; several nozzles under-filling. Traced
to a **partially clogged nozzle manifold** from insufficient between-run sanitation.

## Root cause
Batter residue buildup in the manifold (sanitation SOP interval missed).

## Corrective action
CIP flush of the manifold, replaced two nozzle O-rings, and reset the sanitation
schedule.

## Impact
- **Downtime:** 1.2 hours
- **Cost:** $2,400

## Keywords
depositor, D-4, nozzle clog, deposit weight, sanitation, manifold, muffin batter.
