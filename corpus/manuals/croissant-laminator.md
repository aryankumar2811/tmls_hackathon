# Croissant Laminator — Maintenance Manual (excerpt)

## Sheeting train
The laminator sheets, folds, and gauges dough through a series of paired
rollers. The gauge-roller pair sets final thickness ahead of the freezer.

| Component | Part | Service |
|---|---|---|
| Gauge-roller drive bearing | **BRG-LAM-2200** | replace at 3,500 hrs |
| Roller housing seals | SEAL-LAM-2200 | annually |
| Drive belt | BELT-LAM-2200 | tension 0.7 kN |

## Fault codes
- **E2008** Vibration RMS > 2.5 mm/s on the gauge-roller → suspect bearing.
- **E2009** Freezer compressor cycling abnormally → upstream sheeting issue
  (out-of-spec dough load); inspect laminator gauge gap and bearings.
- **E1003** Lubrication reminder.

## Gauge-roller bearing replacement
1. Lock out the drive and the freezer downstream.
2. Remove the side guard; back off the gauge gap to the maximum.
3. Pull **BRG-LAM-2200** with a bearing puller, press in the replacement.
4. Re-set the gauge gap with a feeler gauge to spec.
5. Run a 30-piece test batch; verify defect rate ≤ 1.0 % and downstream freezer
   no longer raising E2009.
