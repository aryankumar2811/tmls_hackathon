# Spiral Freezer — Maintenance Manual (excerpt)

## Drum drive + refrigeration
JBT FoodTech GYRoCOMPACT spiral with a drum drive bearing and a multi-fan
evaporator coil. Operates at −30 °C chamber temp.

| Component | Part | Service |
|---|---|---|
| Drum drive bearing | **BRG-FRZ-9100-DRV** | lube every 40 days |
| Evaporator coil | COIL-FRZ-9100 | defrost cycle daily; inspect quarterly |
| Door seals | SEAL-FRZ-9100 | inspect monthly |

## Fault codes
- **E1003** Lubrication reminder triggered (scheduled PM).
- **E2009** Freezer compressor cycling abnormally → upstream load issue
  (out-of-spec dough), inspect upstream laminator or sheeter.
- **E2011** Evaporator coil ice buildup excessive → defrost cycle insufficient.

## Lubrication PM (per E1003)
1. Lock out the drum drive.
2. Lubricate **BRG-FRZ-9100-DRV** with the JBT-specified low-temp food-grade
   grease (works at −30 °C without thickening).
3. While the panel is open, inspect door seals and the evaporator coil's frost
   pattern.
4. Reset the lubrication timer.
