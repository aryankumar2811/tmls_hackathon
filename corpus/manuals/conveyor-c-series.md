# Cooling Conveyor C-Series — Maintenance Manual (excerpt)

## Belt tracking
Belt centering is maintained by the tail-pulley squareness and two edge guides.
Target alignment offset is < 2 mm.

| Component | Part |
|---|---|
| Edge guide | **CG-CS-04** |
| Tail-pulley bearing | TPB-CS |
| Drive motor | DM-CS-2 (nominal 9 A) |

## Fault codes
- **C-08** Belt alignment offset > 6 mm → tracking drift; adjust.
- **C-09** Drive motor current > 11 A → belt rubbing frame.

## Belt re-tracking procedure
1. Lock out / tag out the drive.
2. Square the tail pulley; adjust tracking screws ¼ turn at a time.
3. Inspect and replace worn edge guides (**CG-CS-04**).
4. Jog the belt; confirm alignment offset < 2 mm and motor current ≈ 9 A.

> Tracking drift left unaddressed frays the belt edge and can cause full belt
> failure within 8–12 hours.
