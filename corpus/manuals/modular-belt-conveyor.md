# Modular Belt Conveyor — Maintenance Manual (excerpt)

## Drivetrain + belt
Drive-end gearmotor + tail-pulley assembly; modular plastic belt links indexed
by a sprocket pair at each end.

| Component | Part | Service |
|---|---|---|
| Drive-end bearing | **BRG-CNV-1300-DE** | lube every 150 days |
| Tail-pulley bearing | **BRG-CNV-1300-TP** | lube every 150 days |
| Modular belt links | LNK-CNV-1300 | inspect monthly |
| Drive motor | MTR-CNV-1300 | nominal 4 kW |

## Fault codes
- **E1003** Lubrication reminder triggered (scheduled PM).
- **E1004** Belt alignment offset > 5 mm → adjust tracking.
- **E2010** Drive motor current > 8 A → belt rubbing the frame.

## Lubrication PM (per E1003)
1. Apply food-grade NLGI-2 grease to **BRG-CNV-1300-DE** and **BRG-CNV-1300-TP**.
2. Inspect modular belt links for cracks; replace any with hairline fractures.
3. Reset the lubrication timer.
