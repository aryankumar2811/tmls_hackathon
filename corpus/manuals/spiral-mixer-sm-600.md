# Spiral Mixer SM-600 — Maintenance Manual (excerpt)

## Drivetrain
The SM-600 spiral hook is driven through a two-stage gearbox and a drive-end /
non-drive-end bearing set.

| Component | Part | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Drive-end bearing | **BRG-600-DE** | grease every 500 hrs |
| Non-drive-end bearing | BRG-600-NDE | grease every 500 hrs |
| Drive belt | BELT-600 | tension 0.8 kN |

## Fault codes
- **M-11** Vibration RMS > 2.0 mm/s → suspect bearing wear; alert.
- **M-12** Vibration RMS > 2.4 mm/s → failure-imminent; schedule replacement.
- **M-15** Bearing temp > 70 °C.

## Bearing replacement (drive-end)
1. Lock out / tag out; remove the rear guard.
2. Slide the hook coupling; pull **BRG-600-DE** with a bearing puller.
3. Press in the new bearing; re-grease housing with food-grade NLGI-2.
4. Re-tension BELT-600 to 0.8 kN.
5. Run unloaded 10 min; verify vibration RMS ≤ 1.3 mm/s, bearing temp < 58 °C.

## Recommended intervals
- Vibration trend review: weekly.
- Lubrication: every 500 runtime hours.
