# SOP — Tunnel Oven Heating Element Replacement

**Scope:** Replacing a degraded heating element on XB-2400 tunnel ovens.
**Frequency:** As triggered by fault E-21 or predictive alert; inspect every 2,000 hrs.

## Safety
1. Lock out / tag out the oven main disconnect.
2. Allow the chamber to cool below 60 °C before entry.
3. Verify zero energy on the contactor bank with a meter.

## Procedure
1. Identify the affected zone/element from the fault code (e.g. E-21 → Zone 2 top, **HE-2400-Z2**).
2. Remove the zone access panel.
3. Photograph wiring, disconnect bus-bar terminals.
4. Remove and replace the element; torque terminals to **18 N·m**.
5. Recalibrate the zone thermocouple pair.
6. Restore power, run a 30-minute soak, verify current draw within ±0.5 A of nominal
   and thermocouple variance < 3 °C².

## Records
Log part number, technician, downtime, and post-repair readings in the CMMS.

thermocouple calibration.
