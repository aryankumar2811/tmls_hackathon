# SOP — Preventive Lubrication (per E1003)

**Scope:** Scheduled lubrication on any asset whose controller has raised
**E1003 — Lubrication reminder triggered**. Applies to: Robotic Decorator,
Croissant Laminator, Modular Belt Conveyor, Spiral Freezer, CIP System,
Labeling System, Servo Depositor.

**Frequency:** As triggered by E1003, or per the per-asset interval (e.g.
Modular Belt Conveyor 150 days, Spiral Freezer 40 days, Croissant Laminator at
1,000 hours).

## Safety
1. Lock out / tag out the drive.
2. Confirm the asset is at rest and below 50 °C surface temperature.

## Procedure
1. Identify the affected bearing(s) from the equipment manual (e.g.
   **BRG-CNV-1300-DE / -TP**, **BRG-FRZ-9100-DRV**, **BRG-LAM-2200**).
2. Wipe fittings; apply grease until fresh grease appears at the relief.
3. Use food-grade NLGI-2 for ambient drives. Use the OEM-specified low-temp
   grease for freezer drives (works at −30 °C without thickening).
4. Do not over-grease; wipe excess.
5. Inspect adjacent components while the panel is open (belt links, door seals,
   evaporator coil frost pattern, harness condition).
6. Reset the lubrication timer.

## Escalation
If post-lubrication vibration RMS still exceeds the equipment's E2002/E2008
threshold, escalate to bearing replacement per the [bearing-replacement](
bearing-replacement.md) SOP — do not run past the equipment's E3002 threshold.
