# SOP — Spiral Mixer Bearing Lubrication

**Scope:** Scheduled lubrication of SM-600 spiral mixer bearings.
**Frequency:** Every 500 runtime hours, or on vibration alert (fault M-11).

## Safety
1. Lock out / tag out the mixer.
2. Confirm the bowl and hook are stationary.

## Procedure
1. Wipe the grease fittings on the drive-end (**BRG-600-DE**) and non-drive-end bearings.
2. Apply food-grade NLGI-2 grease until fresh grease appears at the relief.
3. Do not over-grease; wipe excess.
4. Inspect the drive belt tension (target 0.8 kN).
5. Record vibration RMS after a 10-minute unloaded run (target ≤ 1.3 mm/s).

## Escalation
If vibration RMS exceeds 2.0 mm/s (M-11) after lubrication, schedule bearing
replacement per the SM-600 manual — do not run past 2.4 mm/s (M-12).
